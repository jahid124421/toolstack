What is Split PDF?

Split PDF is built for anyone who needs to extract pages from a PDF without downloading software or signing up. Whether you're sending a report, filling a form, or archiving paperwork, PDF tasks come up constantly — and paying for a full PDF suite for a one-off job makes little sense. Split PDF solves that in one click, right in your browser — and because the work happens on your own device, it's both instant and private.
