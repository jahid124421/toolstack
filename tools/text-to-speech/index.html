What is Text to Speech?

Text to Speech is built for anyone who needs to read text aloud without downloading software or signing up. Data rarely arrives in the format you need, so quick, reliable conversion saves real time. Text to Speech solves that in one click, right in your browser — and because the work happens on your own device, it's both instant and private.

How to use Text to Speech

  1. Click “Open Text to Speech” to launch the tool.
  2. Paste or type your text into the box.
  3. Pick your options or press the action button.
  4. Copy or download the result instantly.

Common uses

  • Encode or decode data while debugging an app.
  • Switch a color value between HEX, RGB and HSL.
  • Turn a JSON export into CSV for a spreadsheet.
  • Convert units for a recipe, DIY project, or homework.

Frequently asked questions

Is Text to Speech free to use?

Text to Speech is completely free. There are no limits, no watermarks on your results, and no sign-up required.

Yes. Everything runs locally in your browser, so the data you enter never leaves your device.

Do I need to install anything or create an account?

No. Text to Speech works instantly in any modern browser on desktop, tablet or mobile — nothing to download and no account needed.

There's no practical limit — Text to Speech handles your text in the browser, so even large inputs are processed quickly.
